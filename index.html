Day 42

The Morning Arati at Maheshwar

Maheshwar, Madhya Pradesh | March 29, 2026

Distance: 18 km
Terrain: Riverside Stone
Mood: Meditative

Sunrise over the Ahilya Ghat.

The walk today began at 4:30 AM. The air was crisp, and the sound of the Narmada was the only companion until the bells of the temples began to chime. Moving through the stone corridors of the fort, one feels the weight of history and the lightness of the present moment.

"The river does not rush to reach the ocean; it flows because flowing is its nature. Today, I learned that the destination is merely a byproduct of the walk."

Met a group of Parikramavasis from Maharashtra near the Sahastradhara. We shared a simple meal of Poha and tea. Their resilience at age 70 is a testament to the power of 'Shraddha'.

Logistics & Tips

  • Stay: Ahilya Fort Ashram (Clean, high availability).
  • Water: Filtered water available every 2km.
  • Connectivity: Strong 5G signal throughout the town.
